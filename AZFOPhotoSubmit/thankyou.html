Thank you for submitting photos to AZFO 

Your contributions are greatly appreciated.  An email has been automatically generated and it will be sent to you and to the Photo Editors alerting them that your photos have been submitted.  We will get back to you soon (remember we are volunteers!) if more information is needed. 

The email you will receive contains a link to a mockup web page that was automatically generated from your submitted data and photos.  You can follow this link to make sure your images were uploaded and the facts you entered are correct.  Do not worry if some things do not look quite right.   If your photos are selected for posting, we use this page only as a starting point to create the actual web page that will be posted.  The page will be edited and embellished and possibly combined with other submissions. Photos may also be resized, cropped or edited before posting. 

Please be aware that photos submitted to AZFO of Sketch Details Species will be considered by the editors of North American Birds for inclusion in the seasonal reports of that journal and that photos of Review Species will also be considered for review by the Arizona Bird Committee.
